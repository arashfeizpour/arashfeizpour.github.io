Fitness Function:
As my undergrad project I had to write an EMC (Evolutionary Music Composition) program. In this post I want to go over the fitness function calcultion part of the program.
In this program, we have used the cosine used in inner product definition (loaned from IR (information retrieval)) to compare the analysis of a generated chromosome with a target analysis. The complete description of this fitness function is given in the link of the program.

Selection Operator:
In this part, I want to go over the Selection Operator function of the program and how the first step toward a new generation is made and two parents are selected.
In this program, we have used Tournament Selection to select each parent needed between a group of candidate parents. Note the fact that the highest fitness randomly selected parent (candidate) has the highest probabilty of being selected, but, the other ones are also probable.
